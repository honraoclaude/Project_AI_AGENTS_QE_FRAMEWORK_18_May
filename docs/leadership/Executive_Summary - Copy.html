FSC Agentic QE Framework

AI-Powered Quality Engineering for FCA-Regulated Salesforce FSC Delivery
What We Built
The FSC Agentic QE Framework is a purpose-built AI pipeline that automatically applies 53 specialised quality engineering agents to every Jira user story — from initial refinement through production release. Designed specifically for FCA-regulated Salesforce FSC Wealth Management delivery, it produces structured BDD acceptance criteria, FCA compliance checks, test design strategy, risk registers, and audit evidence — in under 10 minutes per story, with no manual effort per sprint.

Business Problem

  • FCA obligations (COBS 9.2, Consumer Duty, FG21/1) missed under sprint pressure
  • Acceptance criteria quality varies by analyst — untestable ACs reach development
  • 30+ hours of senior QE time consumed per sprint on manual documentation
  • Audit evidence assembled retrospectively — not generated at point of delivery

The Solution

  • AI agents review every story automatically when it hits Sprint Ready
  • FCA risk classified as HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW with regulatory triggers identified
  • BDD acceptance criteria generated with Given / When / Then structure
  • Risk register raised at refinement — 2–3 weeks before defects would be found in UAT

FCA Compliance

  • COBS 9.2 — Suitability assessment gate tested on every relevant story
  • Consumer Duty PS22/9 — All four outcomes mapped and evidenced
  • FG21/1 §4.3 — Vulnerable customer pathways verified per story
  • Immutable audit ledger — every agent decision stored, timestamped, and non-deletable
